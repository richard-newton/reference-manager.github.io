Why this tool exists
Reference management without the bloat

Managing references shouldn’t require an account, a cloud service, or a heavyweight application. This manager is built around a simple idea: make creating BibTeX entries effortless.

BibTeX‑first design
Structured forms highlight required and optional fields for each citation type, so you don’t have to keep checking documentation while you write.
Local‑first, no account
Your libraries live on your machine. No sign‑in, no tracking, no forced cloud sync—just files you control.

Welcome
This reference manager grew out of frustration with heavyweight tools and awkward BibTeX workflows. The goal is simple: a focused, BibTeX‑centric app that stays out of your way.
Alpha release notes
The alpha includes: creating and editing BibTeX entries, managing multiple libraries, and attaching PDFs. Future updates will refine the UI, add more citation types, and improve export options.
